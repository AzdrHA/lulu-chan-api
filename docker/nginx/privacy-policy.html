Privacy Policy for Lulu-Chan

We do not collect or store any data related to Lulu-Chan users. No personal information, such as Discord identifiers, messages, or any other data related to bot usage, is recorded.

We do not track the activity of Lulu-Chan users. No information about executed commands, configured settings, or any other action related to bot usage is retained.

Lulu-Chan does not use any cookies to track user activity.

Any changes to this privacy policy will be communicated on our Discord server.

If you have questions or concerns regarding our privacy policy, please contact us at contact@lulu-chan.com.
